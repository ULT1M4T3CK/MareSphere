Our Services

Comprehensive Maritime Solutions

Tailored expertise for modern maritime operations. From technical coordination to emergency response, we provide the specialized services that keep your fleet moving forward.

Nautical-Technical Coordination

Efficient processes, clear interfaces, and technical safety for your fleet.

As interface between shipowners, crew, technical service providers and authorities, MareSphere ensures that nautical-technical processes are implemented in a structured and successful manner.

Our Services Include:

  • Inspection Preparation & Support: Structured planning and preliminary checks for classification, flag state, or customer inspections (e.g., IMCA, OVID).
  • Shipyard & Repair Coordination: Support in planning, coordination and supervision of shipyard stays, conversions or repair projects – on-site or remote.
  • Technical Communication: Clarification of technical questions between ship and shore as well as interface management with external companies.

Your Advantage: You gain time, keep track and get a reliable contact person who makes maritime processes understandable and relieves your daily business – whether for individual projects or ongoing support.

Maritime Emergency Coordination

When things get serious, you need structure and a clear point of contact.

In an emergency, every minute counts – and at the same time you need calm, overview and clean coordination. MareSphere supports small and medium-sized shipowners and individual ship owners to remain capable of acting in critical situations and to initiate the right steps in a sensible order.

Our Services Include:

  • Central Contact Point in Emergencies: You have a fixed point of contact that sorts processes, bundles information and helps to involve the appropriate service providers, authorities and partners.
  • Coordination of Measures: Support in organizing and coordinating technical operations, shipyard contacts, experts or other service providers – with a focus on keeping processes comprehensible and documentable.
  • Documentation & Follow-up: Accompanying support in compiling the essential information and documents, e.g., for insurance, internal evaluation or later reviews.

Important Note: MareSphere does not replace a casualty commander or a specialized authority, but creates structure, relief and overview – especially where internal resources are limited or routine in crisis situations is lacking.

Shipping connects. Even in an emergency, MareSphere ensures that the right information and parties come together – so you don't face complex decisions alone.

Specialized Spare Parts Procurement

Getting the right part to the right place at the right time.

Downtime costs money – especially when a ship is waiting for a special component or technical equipment. MareSphere supports you in procuring critical spare parts quickly, purposefully and with an eye on quality and reliability, and organizing the port call so that operational operations are affected as little as possible.

Our Services Include:

  • Specialized Spare Parts Search: Support in identifying and selecting suitable components, especially for hard-to-find or obsolete parts.
  • Quotation & Order Coordination: Support in obtaining, evaluating and selecting offers as well as coordinating with suppliers and service providers.
  • Port Call-related Coordination: Accompanying organization related to the port call in connection with parts delivery, e.g., coordination with technicians, shipyard or service partners on site.
  • Tracking & Documentation: Transparent communication on status, support with queries and orderly filing of relevant documents for later proof.

Your Advantage: You receive independent, practical support that understands your technical requirements and structures communication between shipowners, suppliers and service providers.

Shipping connects. We help ensure that the right part and the right people come together at the right time – so your ship keeps moving.

Maritime Services (Brokerage)

Reliable partners, clearly coordinated – the right service provider for every need.

Not every shipowner or ship owner has the right service provider "at hand" for every case. This is exactly where MareSphere comes in: As a neutral, experienced partner, MareSphere supports in finding, addressing and structurally involving specialized maritime services, charter ships or tugs as needed.

Our Services Include:

  • Brokerage of Specialized Service Providers: Support in selecting and contacting suitable companies, such as for technical operations, special cleaning, inspections, salvage or support services.
  • Charter & Towing Support: Support in finding suitable charter ships or tugs for specific operations, including coordination of framework conditions and communication.
  • Coordination & Tracking: Structured support from initial contact to implementation, including appointment coordination, information flow and meaningful documentation of agreed services.

MareSphere does not act as a large provider, but as an independent coordinator with maritime practical experience and good access to various actors in the market.

Shipping connects. MareSphere helps bring the right partners together so your projects at sea and in port are reliably implemented.

Documentation & Certificate Management

Keeping your ship documents always "on course".

In modern shipping, the complete maintenance of documents and certificates is crucial – whether for audits, inspections, reflagging or daily operations. MareSphere takes over the professional management of all ship and operationally relevant documents for you.

Our Services Include:

  • We organize and structure your technical documents, certificates and drawings – digital or classic.
  • We keep track of deadlines for inspections, audits and certificate renewals, remind you in time of upcoming measures and actively support preparation and implementation. Just as you like.
  • For reflagging, classification changes, class inspections or flag state inspections and IMCA audits as well as third-party inspections, we accompany you from application to successful acceptance. We check, coordinate and arrange and ensure complete tracking.
  • If desired, we create practical audit templates and checklists from your existing documentation.

Your Advantage: You save time and nerves, avoid unnecessary effort and minimize risks from missing or incorrect documents. MareSphere helps ensure your ship is always ready and well prepared.
