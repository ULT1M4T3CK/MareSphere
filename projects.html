Case Studies

Real-world examples of our maritime expertise in action

Fleet Technical Coordination

Technical Coordination • 6 Months

Challenge: A mid-sized shipping company needed comprehensive technical coordination for their fleet of 8 cargo vessels, managing inspections, certifications, and maintenance schedules across multiple international ports.

Solution: Implemented structured coordination system with centralized documentation management, proactive inspection scheduling, and 24/7 technical support coordination.

Outcome:

  • 100% on-time inspection completion
  • 25% reduction in unplanned downtime
  • Improved classification society ratings

Emergency Response Coordination

Emergency Management • Immediate Response

Challenge: Offshore support vessel experienced critical equipment failure requiring immediate coordination of technical service providers, spare parts procurement, and port logistics.

Solution: Rapid deployment of emergency coordination protocol, sourcing specialized parts from international suppliers, and coordinating technical teams across multiple time zones.

Outcome:

  • 48-hour turnaround from failure to repair
  • Minimized operational disruption
  • Complete documentation for insurance claims

Comprehensive Certificate Management

Documentation Management • Ongoing

Challenge: Vessel owner required complete overhaul of documentation and certification management system to prepare for upcoming classification society audits and flag state inspections.

Solution: Conducted full documentation audit, digitized all certificates and technical documents, implemented tracking system for renewal deadlines, and prepared comprehensive audit packages.

Outcome:

  • Zero non-conformities in inspections
  • Digital document management system
  • Automated renewal tracking

Critical Spare Parts Procurement

Spare Parts • Urgent Delivery

Challenge: Container ship required obsolete engine component with limited global availability, threatening to extend port stay and impact charter commitments.

Solution: Leveraged international network to locate compatible component, arranged expedited logistics, and coordinated installation with certified technicians at next port of call.

Outcome:

  • Component delivered within 72 hours
  • No charter delays or penalties
  • Established alternative supplier relationship
